Use the arrow keys on the keyboard to move the character:

Up arrow -> walk
Left arrow-> turn left
Right arrow-> turn right
Up arrow + shift -> run

The goal is to find the Key...but pay attention to the rabbits, cause they are not kind and little real animals but they are robot bombs.. so don't get too close to them or they explode and your game will be over!

When you tap "New Game" you can set the night sky (by default it's daytime) and the difficulty level (higher level means more rabbits with higher range of danger).
